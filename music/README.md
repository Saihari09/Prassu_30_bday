# Music

Drop your background music file in here.

**Recommended:** name it `background.mp3` so it just works. (That's what `content.js` references by default.)

If you use a different filename or format, update `WISH_MOMENT.music` in `content.js`:

```js
music: 'music/your-file.mp3'
```

## Tips

- **Format:** MP3 works in every browser. M4A and OGG also fine. Avoid WAV (huge files).
- **Size:** Keep under ~5 MB so the page loads fast. A 3–4 minute song around 128–192 kbps is ideal.
- **Volume:** The app sets volume to 40% automatically. Pick a song that sounds good at low volume.
- **Looping:** The track loops automatically when it ends.
- **Autoplay:** Music starts when she taps the candles (browsers require a user gesture before audio plays — the cake tap counts).

## Song ideas

Something instrumental and warm tends to land best — overpowering lyrics can fight the wishes on screen. But if a specific song means something to the two of you, that always wins.
